Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

Bookworm ("we", "our", "the service") is an automated bot on X (Twitter) that facilitates gifting Kindle eBooks between users. The service is operated independently.

2. Data We Collect

  • X (Twitter) usernames — of the sender and recipient, collected from public mentions directed at @bookwormx402.
  • Amazon product links — provided by the sender as part of the gift command.
  • Amazon account email address — collected from the recipient solely to deliver the Kindle book gift.
  • Payment information — processed by our third-party payment provider (Stripe). We do not store card details.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill Kindle book gifts as requested.
  • To communicate transaction status via X replies or DMs.
  • To verify and confirm payment.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

Transaction data (usernames, emails, links) is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the order and resolve any disputes, typically no longer than 90 days.

5. Third-Party Services

  • X (Twitter) — for mention detection and replies. Subject to X's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — for payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon — for eBook fulfillment. Subject to Amazon's Privacy Notice.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us via X DM at @bookwormx402.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach out via X: @bookwormx402.
